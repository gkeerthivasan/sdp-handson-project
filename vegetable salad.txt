        <p id="benifitsParagraph"> Salad greens contain Vitamin A, Vitamin C, beta-carotene, calcium, folate, fiber, and phytonutrients . Leafy vegetables are a good choice for a healthful diet because they do not contain cholesterol and are naturally low in calories and sodium.</p>
            <h3 id="steps">Steps to make Vegetable Salad</h3>
            <ul id="stepForVegetableSalad">
                <li>Wash all the vegetables thoroughly under the running water. Run them dry</li>
                <li>Peel and chop all the vegetables to bit sized pieces. Grate beetroot and carrot. Add them to large serving bowl.</li>
                <li>Deseed the green chilies or crush black pepper and set aside.</li>
                <li>Add all the prepared vegetables , mint and coriander leaves to the bowl. When you are ready to serve the salad, add salt , lemon juice and pepper.</li>
                <li>toss them well. Lastly sprinkle oil and toss.</li>
                <li>Garnish with seeds or nuts. Now the vegetable salad is ready to serve.</li>
            </ul>
